About

MyStopMonitor is an application to help all of us that take public transport in our daily lives, the use of the application is to help remind you that the next stop coming is the one you are going to get off the train at. Because all of us have had the horrible experience of coming to as the train is pulling out of a station and frantically trying to find a sign post or marking that tells you its not the stop you need to get off at!! Now this solution will remind you before that important stop arrives, how is this different from the apple reminders application built into IOS 6+ well it is tailored around the PTV or Melbourne train providers records, so the locations of stations are already there for you, any updates or new stops will automatically be added to your list of stops. This application is made in a way that you can set your stop's both for work/school and home and close the application and when the your getting close to the station set the phone will alert you and ask if you wish to open the app and stop the alert.
